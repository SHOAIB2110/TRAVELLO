Victoria Memorial

The Victoria Memorial is a large marble building in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, which was built between 1906 and 1921. It is dedicated to the memory of Queen Victoria, then Empress of India, and is now a museum and tourist destination under the auspices of the Ministry of Culture. Wikipedia Construction started: 1906 Height: 56 m Founded: 1921 Architectural style: Indo-Saracenic architecture Architects: William Emerson, Vincent Esch
