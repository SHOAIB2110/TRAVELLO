Indian Museum

The Indian Museum in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, also referred to as the Imperial Museum at Calcutta in colonial-era texts, is the ninth oldest museum of the world, oldest museum in India and the largest museum in India. Wikipedia Founded: 1814 Director: Shri Arijit Dutta Choudhury Public transit access: Park Street metro station (Kolkata Metro) Collection size: 102,646 (as of March 31, 2004)
